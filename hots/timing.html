Timing-Übersicht

Mercenaries

Camp Initial spawn time respawn time
Siege Camp 2:00 3:00
Bruiser Camp 2:00 4:00
Boss Camp 3:00 5:00

Blackheart's Bay

Doubloon Camp:

  • Initial spawn time: 2:00
  • Respawn time: 2:30

Chests:

  • Initial spawn time: 0:50
  • Respawn time: 2:30 to 3:15 after last chest has been broken. Time is random

Cursed Hollow

Curse:

  • If there was no curse previously: 0:50 to 1:40 (random)
  • If there was a curse previously: 3:00 to 4:00 (random)

Dragon Shire

Shrines:

  • Shrines opens first at 1:15
  • Shrines open again 2:00 after the dragon is killed

Garden of Terror

Nights:

  • The first night starts at 1:30
  • The next night starts 3:20 after the last plant has been killed

Haunted Mines

Mine:

  • The mine opens at 2:00
  • Minions respawn time in mines: 2:00 after golems have died

Sky Temple

Temples:

  • Initial activation: 1:30
  • Next activation: 2:00 after last temple shot

Order:

  • First activation: top and mid
  • Second activation: bot
  • Third activation: bot and mid or top
  • As of the fourth activation: Random in location and number
